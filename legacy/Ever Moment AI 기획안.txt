# Modify Ever Moment AI design.

This is not a family photo storage app.

It is an AI memory restoration and wedding photo creation app.

Main concept:
Users upload old photos or current photos.
AI recreates missed beautiful moments:
- parents' young days
- wedding photos they never had
- reminder wedding portraits
- restored old memories

Change hero image:
Show emotional AI transformation:
Before old faded photo →
After beautiful wedding portrait.

Use split before/after visual.

Change main headline:

"The Moment You Missed,
Created Again with AI"

Subtitle:
"Restore old memories and create beautiful AI wedding moments for your family."

Main CTA:
"Create Wedding Moment"

Second CTA:
"Restore Old Photo"

Keep:
warm ivory background,
champagne gold,
premium photo studio feeling.

Avoid:
family archive app feeling,
social media app feeling.

Make it feel like:
luxury wedding studio + emotional AI photo service.
